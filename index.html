What We Believe

Our faith is founded on Jesus Christ, the Holy Bible, and the unity of His Church.

The Gospel

Jesus Christ died for our sins, was buried, and rose again. Through Him we receive forgiveness, salvation, and eternal life by grace through faith.

The Bible

The Holy Bible is the inspired Word of God. It is our foundation for faith, spiritual growth, and everyday living.

The Church

We believe the Church is the family of God, called to worship Christ, make disciples, and share His love with the world.
